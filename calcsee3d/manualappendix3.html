  • A3: The function parser grammar

<expr>: <expr> + <term> | <expr> - <term> | <term>

<term>: <term> * <factor> | <term> - <factor> | <factor>

<factor>: <factor> ^ <exp> | <exp>

<exp>: cos(<expr>) | sin(<expr>) | pow(<expr> , <expr>) | (<expr>) | e | <number>

<number>: any decimal number.
